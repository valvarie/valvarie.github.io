Education

I have learnt more in 10 days of EYP then in 15 years of school - said one of the delegates I have chaired. It has happened that most of the competences I possess and predict to be using professionally come from European Youth Parliament. Not only have I acquired understanding of how European Union functions, deepen my appreciation for European values, reopened European identity in me and gained insight on contemporary problems of global society, I have learnt very basic skills. EYP has taught me to express my ideas in writing, compromise and communicate in multicultural environment. EYP has given me a chance to organize projects with 100+ participants, manage teams over short and long periods of time, coordinate projects. I believe I learnt to love people through EYP.
